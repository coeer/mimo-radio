# Kimi Design System

A late-night radio aesthetic for an AI DJ companion app.

## Brand Essence

**One sentence:** Kimi is the DJ who knows your fourteen years of playlists.

**Tone:** Warm, intimate, soulful — like tuning into a familiar late-night radio station at 2 AM.

**Differentiation:** Not a cold music player. Not a chatbot. A *companion* who curates music with taste and talks to you like a friend who happens to have perfect musical intuition.

## Sources

- Original concept: Douyin video by mmguo (Kimi/Claude AI Radio)
- Codebase: `ai-radio/` project root
- Aesthetic direction: Frontend-design skill — committed to "late-night radio" extreme direction

## Tokens

See `./tokens/colors_and_type.css` for the full token set.

Key principles:
- **No bluish-purple gradients** — exhausted industry default, replaced with warm amber/copper
- **Deep charcoal base** — `#050505` to `#0e0e12`, never flat black
- **Warm accents** — the "radio glow" at `#d4a574`
- **Space Grotesk** for display, system Chinese fonts for body
- **Noise + radial glow** background treatments, not flat colors
